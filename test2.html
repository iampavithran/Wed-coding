JOKE

A JOKE is a display of humour in which words are used within a specific
and well-defined narrative structure to make people laugh and is usually
not meant to be interpreted literally. It usually takes the form of a
story, often with dialogue, and ends in a punch line, whereby the
humorous element of the story is revealed; this can be done using a pun
or other type of word play, irony or sarcasm, logical incompatibility,
